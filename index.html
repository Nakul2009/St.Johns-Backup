What's New ?

  • School Sanitation

    School Sanitation and Hygiene Education (SSHE), is globally recognised as a key intervention to promote children's right to health and clean environment and to influence a generational change in health promotional behaviour and attitudes.

    Our school advocates the practice of ensuring all children, teaching and non-teaching staff strictly follow all safety measures and hygiene standards as per the government norms .

  • Our Library

    The School has a well established library having more than fifteen thousand valuable books covering all subjects of study and reference. The library is accommodated in a spacious, well ventilated hall with reading rooms.

    Library - Working Hours / Rules and Regulations
    Library is kept open from 9.30 a.m. to 5.00 p.m. on all working days. Students are taken to the library class-wise during their library periods and also during the free periods under principal's supervision.
